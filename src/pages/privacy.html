Privacy policy

This is a simple, static information site about Cambridge punting tours. We collect as little as possible.

What we collect

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not run a contact form. If you email us at info@puntingtourscambridge.com, we receive whatever you put in that email and use it only to reply to you.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand, in aggregate, which pages people find useful. It sets cookies and collects standard, non-identifying usage data such as pages viewed and rough location. We do not use it to identify you personally.

Affiliate links and bookings

When you click through to GetYourGuide or Viator and book, the booking and any personal or payment details are handled by that platform under their own privacy policies, not by us. See our affiliate disclosure for how those links work.

Your choices

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings, and you can use a browser-level "do not track" or analytics opt-out at any time.
