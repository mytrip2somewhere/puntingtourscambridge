What this site is

Punting Tours Cambridge (puntingtourscambridge.com) is an independent local-knowledge guide to punting tours on the River Cam in Cambridge, England. It helps visitors choose and book punting tours through the operators' official GetYourGuide and Viator listings. It is not a single operator, and it is not the river authority.

Who runs it

The pages are written by Jordan Harrington, a Cambridge punting guide since 2021 who has guided more than 1,000 visitors along the River Cam. Jordan specialises in the College Backs and writes from the back of the punt, pole in hand, rather than from a brochure. The "1,000+ visitors guided since 2021" figure is the guide's own record, presented as such.

Core facts, stated plainly

  • Tours run on the River Cam along the College Backs, the stretch of river behind the historic university colleges.
  • Colleges you pass include King's, Clare, Trinity, St John's and Queens'.
  • Famous landmarks on the route include the Bridge of Sighs, the Mathematical Bridge and Clare Bridge.
  • A guided tour lasts about 45 to 50 minutes.
  • Cambridge has six licensed punting stations, licensed by the Conservators of the River Cam.
  • Punting runs year-round except Christmas Day, and is busiest in summer.
