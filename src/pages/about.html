Who is Jordan?

Jordan Harrington is a Cambridge-based punting guide with deep roots in the city. Since 2021 he has led guided punting tours along the River Cam, specialising in the world-famous College Backs. Born and raised in Cambridge, he pairs expert local knowledge with a real passion for the city's history.

He navigates traditional punts while giving engaging, well-researched commentary on the University's historic colleges, its famous bridges, and centuries of academic tradition. Over the years he has guided more than 1,000 international visitors, for individuals, couples, families and small groups, with a focus on historical accuracy, storytelling and guest comfort. That trip-after-trip repetition is why the detail on this site is specific: which slot has the best light on King's College Chapel, where the river gets busy, and what makes a tour go from good to memorable.

The "1,000+ visitors since 2021" figure is the guide's own record, presented as his number rather than an independent audit.

What is Punting Tours Cambridge?

This is an independent local-knowledge guide to punting on the River Cam. It is not a single operator and it does not own a fleet of punts. Jordan guides on the river under several agencies, so the site can compare the tours honestly rather than push one company.

When you decide to book, we send you to the operator's own official listing on GetYourGuide or Viator, where you complete the booking and pay. Your money goes straight to the people on the water. We do not take your payment, we do not hold your booking, and we never invent a tour that is not real. The job of this site is to tell you which of the genuine, operator-run tours fits your group, your budget and your day, and then hand you to the official page to finish.

How this site is kept honest

Everything here is built to be checked, not taken on trust. Three commitments keep it that way.

Real ratings, creditedStar ratings and review counts are the operators' own figures from their GetYourGuide and Viator listings, and we say which listing each one comes from. We do not invent or round up numbers.
Live pricesPrices change by season, date and group size, so we show typical ranges here and link you to the official listing to see the live price before you pay.
Affiliate disclosureWhen you book through a link on this site, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. It never changes which tour we recommend. Full details are on our disclosure page.
