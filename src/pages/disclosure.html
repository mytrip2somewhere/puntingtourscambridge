Affiliate disclosure

Punting Tours Cambridge is an independent guide. Some of the links on this site are affiliate links, and we want to be upfront about that.

How we earn

When you book a punting tour through one of our links to GetYourGuide or Viator, we may earn a small commission, at no extra cost to you. You pay the same price you would pay going to the listing directly. Those commissions are what let us keep the site free and independent.

Honesty commitments

Ratings and review counts shown are the operators' own current figures from their GetYourGuide and Viator listings, credited to their source. Prices on those platforms are dynamic, so we point you to the live listing for today's exact price rather than printing a figure that goes stale. We only link to real, operator-run tours described on this site. A commission never changes which tour we recommend, and we say so wherever a link earns us one.

We are an independent local-knowledge guide, not the tour operator and not the river authority. Where we describe the route, the colleges and the bridges, we report what you actually see on the water.

This disclosure is provided in line with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission's guidance on endorsements and affiliate relationships, and UK advertising standards on clearly identifying paid links. A short version of it also appears in the footer of every page.
