About these guides

Which punting guide should I read first?

Start with the prices guide so you know the going rate for shared, private and self-hire punts, then read the College Backs route guide to see exactly which colleges and bridges you pass. Those two cover the questions most visitors ask before they book.

Are these guides independent?

Yes. This is an independent guide site written by Cambridge punting guide Jordan Harrington. Ratings and prices are the operators' own figures from their official GetYourGuide and Viator listings, and we earn a commission only when you book through a link, which never changes which tour we recommend.

Do the guides help me choose between the tours?

They do. The guides explain the real differences between shared, private and student-guided punts, and the comparison page lines all of them up side by side so you can pick the one that fits your group and budget.
