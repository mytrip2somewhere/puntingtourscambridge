## 3. University of Cambridge calendar

**Source:** https://www.cam.ac.uk/about-the-university/term-dates-and-calendars

2026 to 2027 academic year, Full Term (the 8-week teaching period):
- **Michaelmas 2026:** Full Term 6 October to 4 December 2026. Term 1 October to 19 December 2026.
- **Lent 2027:** Full Term 19 January to 19 March 2027. Term 5 January to 25 March 2027.
- **Easter 2027:** Full Term 27 April to 18 June 2027. Term 17 April to 25 June 2027.

**Graduation / General Admission 2026:** Wednesday 1 July to Saturday 4 July 2026, four Congregations,
10am each day.
Source: https://www.cambridgestudents.cam.ac.uk/graduation/degree-ceremonies/degree-ceremony-dates

**May Week:** the end-of-year celebration period (May Balls, June Events, garden parties). Despite the
name it happens in **June**, after Easter Term exams. The name stuck after the events shifted from May
in 1882.
Sources: https://www.alumni.cam.ac.uk/story/the-evolution-of-may-week ,
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/May_Ball_in_Cambridge

**College closures during exams:** varies college by college; there is no single university-wide
schedule. Queens' College publishes one and states it is open all year round except during the
Examination period, listing **19 April to 4 July 2026**, plus certain ceremonial days and the Christmas
holidays. https://www.queens.cam.ac.uk/visiting-the-college/tourist-information/
King's College notes it may restrict visitor access "particularly during exam period" but publishes no
blanket exam-term closure for the Chapel. https://www.kings.cam.ac.uk/opening-times-and-tickets

## 6. Established site facts reused (from `site.config.md` / operator input)

- Punting runs year round, every day except Christmas Day.
- A chauffeured Backs punt runs about 45 to 50 minutes.
- Tours usually still run in light rain, often with covered punts and blankets.
- Summer is busiest on the river; late spring and early autumn are calmer.
