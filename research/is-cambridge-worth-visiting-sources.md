## Established site facts reused (already verified elsewhere in this repo)

- A chauffeured College Backs punt runs about 45 to 50 minutes.
- The Backs run past King's, Clare, Trinity, St John's, the Bridge of Sighs and the Mathematical Bridge.
- The Conservators of the River Cam license six punting stations.

- **King's College Chapel admission price, college entry prices, punt prices.** Dynamic. Page
  states ranges/behaviour only and routes to live official listings, per project rules.
- **"Prettier" as a fact.** The ChatGPT query "which is prettier, cambridge or oxford?" is a
  matter of taste. The page answers it honestly as an opinion clearly framed as one, and
  restricts the *factual* comparison to verified geography, dates and travel times.

  wide enough that it was not used.
