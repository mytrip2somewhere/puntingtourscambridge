# 00-project-overview.md — Phase 0: Strategy & Foundation

Generic. Read site specifics from `site.config.md`. Global rules live in root `CLAUDE.md`.

## PURPOSE
Establish goals, audience, and positioning before any research or building. This frames
every later phase.

## GOALS
- Primary KPI: affiliate clicks / bookings to GetYourGuide (GYG) and Viator to a real travel operator.
- Secondary: rank on Google and other search engines; get cited by LLMs (ChatGPT,
  Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini).

## MODEL
Affiliate content site that send links to a real operator Oleg working for. Conversion happens on product
cards routing to the affiliate links Oleg provides. (See `site.config.md` > MODEL.)

## AUDIENCE / BUYER JOURNEY
Serve all stages: dreaming (inspiration), researching (guides, facts), comparing
(comparison pages), booking (money pages → affiliate links). Personas vary per
destination; infer from the destination in `site.config.md` and the Phase 1 research
(e.g. pre-trip planners vs in-destination last-minute bookers, solo vs group vs family).

## POSITIONING
Independent local-knowledge guide, not the operator and not a generic listicle farm.
Honest, specific, a little opinionated, genuinely helpful. Affiliate links live inside
useful content, never shouting.

## EMD NOTE
If the domain is an exact-match domain (see `site.config.md` > IDENTITY), the domain
already owns the head term. Weight pages toward specific long-tail terms rather than
re-targeting the exact-match phrase everywhere.

## HANDOFF
This framing feeds Phase 1 (research) and Phase 2 (architecture).
